# 🔓 Social Reinsertion, Reintegration, and Disciple Formation of Detainees

**Organization:** Open Hearts Training Center (OHTC) - Cap-Haitian, Haiti

> Restoring dignity and building futures for 1,500+ women and children in Haitian prisons through vocational training, spiritual guidance, and social reintegration.


## 📋 Project Description

This transversal project provides **social, psychological, and spiritual support** to at least **1,500 women and children** detained in the prisons of Cap-Haitian, Grand Rivière du Nord, and Fort Liberté. It equips detainees with professional and technical skills to reintegrate peacefully into society and pursue sustainable livelihoods after release.

The project operates at three levels:

- **Social** — Guiding detainees toward peaceful reintegration into their communities
- **Psychological** — Helping individuals reconcile with themselves and process trauma
- **Spiritual** — Supporting reconciliation with God and formation as disciples

By engaging detainees in vocational training and career orientation, the project contributes directly to **reducing rates of crime and social violence** in northern Haiti and aligns participants with Haiti's sustainable development goals.

## 🎯 Objectives

| # | Objective |
|---|-----------|
| 1 | Train **30 professionals** in Social Reinsertion methodology |
| 2 | Assist at least **1,500 women and children** detained per year |
| 3 | Provide **professional and vocational training** to detainees |
| 4 | Distribute at least **3,000 kits** per year to detainees |
| 5 | Introduce women and children to **diverse career pathways** |
| 6 | Engage detainees in projects addressing **real-life community challenges** |
